2. Collection and Use of Data

The app does not share health or fitness data with any third parties. If you choose to manually export data (e.g., to a file, iCloud Drive, or email), this is entirely initiated by you.

5. User Rights

You can change or revoke the app’s access to HealthKit data at any time in iOS system settings:
Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → [App Name]
